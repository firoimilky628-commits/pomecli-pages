PomeCLI

PomeCLI is an iPhone and iPad terminal client for connecting to your own Mac or server over SSH and operating tmux sessions.

PomeCLI does not provide a hosted SSH service. You bring and manage your own SSH server, credentials, network, and tmux environment.
